About ISTE TSEC

Our Story

ISTE Student's Chapter TSEC MH-116 is a prestigious, nationally acclaimed society, proudly affiliated with ISTE New Delhi. We are dedicated to fostering technical excellence and innovation, providing a dynamic platform for students to develop unparalleled expertise in technical skills, leadership, and management capabilities.

With a strong focus on holistic personality development, ISTE TSEC MH-116 equips students with the tools and confidence to thrive in a highly competitive and technologically driven global environment.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, foster innovation, and develop leadership qualities through various activities, workshops, and competitions.

Our Vision

To be the leading student chapter in technical education, recognized for excellence in innovation, leadership, and community service.
